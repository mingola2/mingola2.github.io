Mingola is a senior graphic design major at the University of Massachusetts: Dartmouth. She began her education with a passion for poster and information design, but has come to develop a love for brand identity, UX/UI, and web design. Influenced by traditional mediums she enjoys combining illustration and the fine arts into her designs. Mingola is proud to have been given the opportunity to partner with Anime Boston and the Center for Women, Gender, and Sexuality to collaborate and design promotional material. Driven and motivated, she is currently looking for a summer internship to help her learn, flourish and grow.
